Projects



Anime Info Hub

For the Anime Info Hub project, I delved into the captivating world of anime to create a comprehensive platform for enthusiasts. The website serves as an information hub, offering users a seamless experience as they explore the vast realm of anime. From detailed reviews and ratings to personalized recommendations, Anime Info Hub becomes a go-to destination for both seasoned fans and newcomers to the anime universe. With a user-friendly interface and a commitment to providing accurate and engaging content, this project reflects my passion for technology and love for the enchanting narratives found in anime.



Lyrical - Indian Hip Hop Updates

Lyrical, my project dedicated to Indian Hip Hop updates, emerged from a deep appreciation for the vibrant and evolving hip-hop culture in India. This platform provides a space for enthusiasts to stay connected with the latest news, releases, and insights into the Indian hip-hop scene. With a focus on user engagement and community building, Lyrical fosters a sense of connection among fans and artists alike. Through this project, I've not only showcased my technical skills but also contributed to the celebration and documentation of the rich cultural tapestry that is Indian hip-hop



Traveler's Guide

The Traveler's Guide project represents my exploration into the intersection of technology and wanderlust. Designed to assist travelers in planning their next adventure, this platform goes beyond conventional travel guides. It incorporates user reviews, curated lists of must-visit places, and a personalized approach to help individuals craft memorable journeys. From hidden gems to popular destinations, Traveler's Guide serves as a virtual companion for those seeking inspiration and practical guidance. Through this project, I aimed to create a dynamic and user-centric space that not only facilitates trip planning but also fosters a sense of community among fellow travelers.
